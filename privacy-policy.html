Privacy Policy — InvestHelper

Effective date: 2026-05-16

InvestHelper (“the app”, “we”) is a tool that lets you track shared physical-asset investments (cars, bikes, property, electronics, etc.) together with friends and calculate each person’s share of the profit or loss. This document describes what data we collect, why, and your rights over that data.

Contact: techasm.dev@gmail.com

1. What we collect

We collect only the data you give us directly. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting.

Account data

  • Your email address (required to sign in)
  • A password hash (your password itself is never stored or transmitted in plain text after authentication)
  • An optional display name
  • Account creation and last-sign-in timestamps

App data you enter

  • Deals you create — title, asset type (car/bike/etc.), purchase price, sale price, expenses, notes, currency, dates
  • Investors you add to a deal — display name (a label you type), invested amount, agreed return %, paid/unpaid status
  • Expenses you add to a deal — label and amount
  • App preferences — theme mode (light/dark/system) and default currency

Technical data

  • Authentication tokens managed by Supabase (used to keep you signed in)
  • Server-side request logs kept by our hosting provider, Supabase (used for security and abuse prevention)

We do not collect: contacts, location, microphone, camera, photos, files outside the app, advertising identifiers, device identifiers, biometrics.

2. Why we collect it

PurposeData used
Provide the core service (let you record deals and see calculations)App data you enter
Identify your account across devicesEmail, password hash, auth tokens
Protect against abuse and unauthorized accessAuth tokens, server-side logs

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to build profiles for marketing.

3. Where it’s stored

All app data and authentication records are stored by Supabase Inc., our backend provider, on managed PostgreSQL infrastructure. You can review Supabase’s own privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

Data is stored in the cloud region selected for the project. The project owner controls the region.

4. Who can see your data

  • You, when signed in to your account
  • Database administrators of the project owner (currently the developer) — strictly for technical maintenance, debugging at your request, or to comply with law
  • Supabase Inc., as our infrastructure provider, in the same minimal capacity needed to operate the service
  • Law enforcement, only when required by a valid legal process

The names you type for “investor” entries are labels you choose — those people are not contacted by the app, do not receive emails, and have no account unless they separately sign up themselves.

5. How long we keep it

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. When you delete your account from within the app (Settings → Delete account), all of the following are permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your authentication record (email, password hash, sessions)
  • Your profile
  • All deals you own
  • All investors and expenses attached to those deals
  • Your app preferences

Server-side request logs kept by Supabase may persist for up to 90 days for security purposes and are then deleted.

6. Your rights

  • Access: you can see all data tied to your account at any time within the app.
  • Correction: you can edit deals, investors, and expenses in-app.
  • Deletion: you can delete your account from Settings → Delete account. This is irreversible.
  • Export: if you need a copy of your data in machine-readable form, email techasm.dev@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.
  • Withdraw consent: by deleting your account or by not using the app.

If you are in the EU/UK, you also have rights under the GDPR/UK GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

7. Security

  • All traffic between the app and our backend uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are never stored — only secure hashes managed by Supabase Auth.
  • Row-level security is enforced on every database table so that one user cannot read another user’s data.
  • We do not request any device permission (location, contacts, etc.) beyond standard network access.

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security issue, please email us at techasm.dev@gmail.com before disclosing publicly.

8. Children

InvestHelper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the “Effective date” at the top and surface a notice in the app the next time you sign in. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
